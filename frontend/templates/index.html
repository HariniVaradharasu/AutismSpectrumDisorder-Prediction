Autism Spectrum Disorder

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects communication, behavior, and social interactions. It is called a "spectrum" disorder because the symptoms and severity vary widely among individuals.

Some common characteristics of ASD include difficulties in social interaction, repetitive behaviors, and unique strengths such as attention to detail and deep focus on specific interests.

Early diagnosis and intervention can help individuals with autism lead fulfilling lives by providing tailored support and therapies.
